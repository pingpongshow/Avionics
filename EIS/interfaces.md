# EIS Interface Specification

## Outputs To MFD

- engine summary heartbeat
- RPM
- manifold pressure
- oil pressure
- oil temperature
- fuel flow
- bus voltage
- fuel quantity by tank
- max `CHT`
- max `EGT`
- per-cylinder `CHT/EGT`
- caution/warning bitmasks

## Configuration Inputs

- cylinder count
- tank count / labels
- range limits per channel
- total usable fuel
